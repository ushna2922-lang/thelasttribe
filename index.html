Our Tribal Heritage

Located at the prestigious Olympus Mall rooftop in F-11, TLT (The Last Tribe) is more than just a restaurant; it’s a tribute to ancestral cooking methods. We combine traditional charcoal grilling with modern culinary finesse.


Whether it’s our signature slow-cooked meats or our hand-crafted artisan breads, every dish tells a story of tradition, passion, and the finest ingredients Pakistan has to offer.
